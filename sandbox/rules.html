SERVER GUIDELINES

Don't do dumb shit that would go against conventional thinking. If it seems like its hurtful to others, the server, or just seems like a bad idea.

Propkill, proppush, propblock, propclimb, propspam, etc., is not allowed.

When making your base, make sure its possible to enter, with a keypad on both sides of the entrance door. Bases do not generally have any limits on doors or the amount of space they take up, but keep the ability for others to raid your base fun and possible, and don't have it negatively impact the experience of others. In addition, keeping your spawn in someone's base, or spawning in props/entities without permission is not allowed.

The use of Wiremod/E2/Starfall in a way that negatively affects the way others play is not allowed. If it seems unbalanced, unoptimized, or something you wouldn't fight against, don't use it.

The use of an NSFW PAC (stuff considered 18+), or a playermodel smaller than the regular citizen playermodel is not allowed (this includes Outfitter).

Don't cheat, plain and simple. The use of anything that isn't intended to be used in-game that gives you an advantage (like cheats), including bugs & server/addon exploits, is not allowed.
